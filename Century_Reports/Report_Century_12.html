(From the Goodreads) Goodreads.com is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love.

The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinions with their friends. The website also provides extensive information on books from all genres and centuries; it also provides the users with rankings, available by different parameters. Summary of the Best books in Century 12

Goodreads provides its users with information on 61 books from Century 12 that have been published by Author/Authors from Geoffrey of Monmouth to Bernard de Ventadour.

The best book in Century 12 was The History of the Kings of Britain by Geoffrey of Monmouth which gathered 30 votes, 2960 scores and an average rating of 4.5.

The book can be found under the following link: “https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/129521.The_History_of_the_Kings_of_Britain”
