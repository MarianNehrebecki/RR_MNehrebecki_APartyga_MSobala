Overview

(From the Goodreads) Goodreads.com is the worlds largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love.

The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinions with their friends. The website also provides extensive information on books from all genres and centuries; it also provides the users with rankings, available by different parameters. The rankings that are of interest to us, are the rating by the century. Below you can find a short summary of each centurys best books.

A few things we can do on Goodreads

  • See what books our friends are reading.
  • Track the books we are reading, have read, and want to read.
  • Check out our personalized book recommendations. The recommendation engine analyzes 20 billion data points to give suggestions tailored to our literary tastes.
  • Find out if a book is a good fit for us from our communitys reviews.

Summary of the Best books in Century 21

Goodreads provides its users with information on 9082 books from Century 21 that have been published by Author/Authors from J.K. Rowling to Arthur C. Clarke.

The best book in Century 21 was Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (Harry Potter, #7) by J.K. Rowling which gathered 4090 votes, 404621 scores and an average rating of 5.

The book can be found under the following link: https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/136251.Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows
