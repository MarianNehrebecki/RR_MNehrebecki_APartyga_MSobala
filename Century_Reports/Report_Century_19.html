(From the Goodreads) Goodreads.com is the worlds largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love.

The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinions with their friends. The website also provides extensive information on books from all genres and centuries; it also provides the users with rankings, available by different parameters. Summary of the Best books in Century 19

Goodreads provides its users with information on 1562 books from Century 19 that have been published by Author/Authors from Jane Austen to Guy de Maupassant.
