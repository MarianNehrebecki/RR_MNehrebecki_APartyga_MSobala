(From the Goodreads) Goodreads.com is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love.

The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinions with their friends. The website also provides extensive information on books from all genres and centuries; it also provides the users with rankings, available by different parameters. Summary of the Best books in Century 18

Goodreads provides its users with information on 344 books from Century 18 that have been published by Author/Authors from Jonathan Swift to Frances Brooke.

The best book in Century 18 was Gulliver’s Travels: Travels into Several Remote Nations of the World. by Jonathan Swift which gathered 262 votes, 25800 scores and an average rating of 4.7.

The book can be found under the following link: “https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/7733.Gulliver_s_Travels”
