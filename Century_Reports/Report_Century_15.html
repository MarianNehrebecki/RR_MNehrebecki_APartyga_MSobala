The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinions with their friends. Summary of the Best books in Century 15

Goodreads provides its users with information on 69 books from Century 15 that have been published by Author/Authors from Thomas Malory to Luigi Pulci.

The best book in Century 15 was Le Morte d’Arthur: King Arthur and the Legends of the Round Table by Thomas Malory which gathered 82 votes, 8130 scores and an average rating of 4.7.

The book can be found under the following link: “https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/672875.Le_Morte_d_Arthur”
