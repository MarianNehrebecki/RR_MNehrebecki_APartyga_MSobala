Overview

(From the Goodreads) Goodreads is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love. Goodreads launched in January 2007.

A Few Things We Can Do On Goodreads

  • See what books our friends are reading.
  • Track the books we’ re reading, have read, and want to read.
  • Check out our personalized book recommendations. The recommendation engine analyzes 20 billion data points to give suggestions tailored to our literary tastes.
  • Find out if a book is a good fit for us from our community’s reviews.

Centries 4 summary

Century 4 of Goodreads consisted of 55 books that have been published by Author/Authors starting with Augustine of Hippo and ending with Vegetius. The best book by 4 century: Confessions by Augustine of Hippo gathered 61 votes, 6089 scores and 4.6 average rating.

The above literature items can be found on the website:

“https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/27037.Confessions”.
