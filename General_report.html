Overview

(From the Goodreads) Goodreads.com is the worlds largest site for readers and book recommendations. The mission of Goodreads is to help people find and share books they love.

The users can rate their favourite books, create lists and share their opinion with their friends. The website also provides extensive information on books from all genres and centuries; it also provides the users with rankings, available by different parameters. The rankings that are of interst to us, are the rating by the century. Below you can find a short summary of each centurys best books.

A few things we can do on Goodreads

  • See what books our friends are reading.
  • Track the books we re reading, have read, and want to read.
  • Check out our personalized book recommendations. The recommendation engine analyzes 20 billion data points to give suggestions tailored to our literary tastes.
  • Find out if a book is a good fit for us from our communitys reviews.

Summary of the best books of all time

In total, we gathered available data from Best Books By Century, namely 19471 unique book titles along with their:

  • author
  • total score (which is based on number of votes and and how highly those voters ranked the book)
  • URL link
  • average rating
  • number of times rated

Visualisations:

Books that were rated the most times

The book that was rated the most times is Harry Potter and the Sorcerers Stone (Harry Potter, #1) written by J.K. Rowling which was rated 8525498 number of times.

Books with the highest total scores

The top rated book of all time is To Kill a Mockingbird which was rated 5277975 number of times and received an average rating of 4.27.

Most prolific authors

Stephen King is the author who has written the most books (68) that were present in the Best Books by Century on Goodreads.
