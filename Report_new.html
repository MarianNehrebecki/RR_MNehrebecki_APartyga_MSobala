Summary of the Best Books by Century - Century 4

Goodreads provides its users with information on 54 books from Century 4 that have been published by Author/Authors from Augustine of Hippo to Vegetius.

The best book in Century 4 was Confessions by Augustine of Hippo which gathered 61 votes, 6089 scores and an average rating of 4.6.

The book can be found under the following link: https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/27037.Confessions
